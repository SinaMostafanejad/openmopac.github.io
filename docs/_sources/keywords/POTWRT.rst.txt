.. _POTWRT:

``POTWRT``
==========

In an ESP calculation, write out surface points and electrostatic
potential values to |$<\! $|\ filename\ |$\! >$|.esp in the format:

| Line 1    : Number of points
| Line2 on:  Potential (volts), x, y, z coordinate (in Angstroms)  (4
  numbers)  Format: "1x,4e16.7"
